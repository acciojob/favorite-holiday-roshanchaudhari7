Maharashtra


Maharashtra, is one of the most industrialised states in India. The state's capital, Mumbai, is India's financial and commercial capital From the bustling metropolis of Mumbai to the serene beaches of the Konkan coast, Maharashtra offers a unique blend of urban and natural attractions that cater to all types of travelers.

Top 5 attractions

  1. Lonavala: Lonavala is famous for its trekking expeditions. The most popular trek is to Lohagad Fort, which offers a mix of history and adventure.
  2. Mahabaleshwar: A beautiful hill station known for its scenic viewpoints and strawberries.
  3. Matheran: Matheran is a picturesque hill station located in the Raigad district of Maharashtra, India.
  4. Shirdi: The holy town associated with the revered saint, Sai Baba.
  5. Gateway of India: This iconic monument in Mumbai is a must-visit.

Must-try foods

  • Modak: A sweet dumpling that is especially popular during the Ganesh Chaturthi festival.
  • Puran Poli: A sweet flatbread stuffed with lentil and jaggery filling.
  • Misal Pav: A spicy curry made from sprouted lentils and served with pav.
  • Vada Pav: Often referred to as the Indian burger, it's a popular street food.
  • Pav Bhaji: A spicy vegetable mash served with buttered bread rolls.
